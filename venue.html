The Music Hall


This hall is perfectly suited for any formal or informal event: parties, performances, lectures, ceremonies: you name it! The stage and the seating can be set in any desired arrangement and all technical equipment is at hand. This space also has a private bar which is available for use.

The Theatre


This space is ofcourse loved by thespians and lecturers alike. Perfectly suited for small-scale theatre performances, intimate dance or music recitals, lectures, panel-discussions and even cinema! With the help from our crew you can set up the theatre in a way that will express care and professionalism to your visitors.

What's Included?
Equipment

Both halls are fully equiped with all the tech you might need for your event. All categories of equipment - sound, lighing, projection and furniture - are included in the rental price. For a detailed list of equipment please see this list.

Host

During your event the host will be your go to person for any questions you might have. They are the organizational spindle and will do everything to make sure your event runs smoothly. They are there at the beginning of your event to great you and your visitors with a smile and they'll wave you off at the end of your event.

Technical Staff

Our technical staff consists of formally schooled theatre technicians with a wealth of experience. Your event will be asigned one or two technicians and they will make sure all equipment is installed properly and according to your whishes. We alocate some time before the start for your event so you and the technicians can walk through the event and complete the setup with ease.

Catering

We have a cafe in our building which is affiliated with the venue space. Your guests can grab a coffee or some food in the cafe before or after your event. It's also possible to have some organized drinks here, though not privately. The cafe can also serve catering in the Musichall.this list.
