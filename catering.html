The Bell Café

You'll never feel more at home.

The Bell Jar and The Bell Café go hand in hand. Together we can give you our all-round service. Your guests can grab a coffee or some food in the cafe before or after your event. It's also possible to have some organized drinks here, though not privately. The cafe can also serve catering in the Musichall. We have a standard catering menu, but you can also give our cafe manager Jared Oaks a call to discuss the possibility of some off-menu catering. You can reach him at +41 50 213 45 87 or at cafe@thebelljar.org.
